Dataset: watertemp_may.csv

Description: Mean water temperature in epilimnion of 
   Saidenbach reservoir in May. The data set is intended as course 
   example and may have been supplemented or otherwise modified.
   Please consult Lakebase (https://fred.igb-berlin.de/) 
   for original data if needed for research questions.
